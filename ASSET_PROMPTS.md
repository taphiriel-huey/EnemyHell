## Burning Village Background

### Gemeinsame Stilnotizen

- Dunkle, erwachsene Fantasy, aber nicht edgy oder uebertrieben grimdark.
- Alte RPG-Cover / Fantasy-Poster statt Pixel-Retro oder Mobile-Game-Look.
- Blackhaven-Farbtemperatur: tiefer Charcoal, Weinrot, Ember-Orange, Aschebraun, kaltes Arkanblau.
- Kampfzone muss seitlich lesbar bleiben: klare horizontale Flaechen, wenig Detail im aktiven unteren Drittel.
- Magie als Kampfkunst: kontrollierte Gesten, ruhige Praesenz, keine Anime-Actionpose.
- Gegner muessen auch als kleine Silhouetten unterscheidbar sein.

### Moodboard Prompt

Create a high-quality dark fantasy concept art moodboard for a 2D side-view action roguelite called "Blackhaven Enemyhell". One wide 16:9 image divided into three cinematic panels, not pixel art, not mobile-game style, no silly wizard hats. Panel 1: key art of a battle-hardened stylish mage in a grim fantasy village, inspired by the controlled cool of Neo, Morpheus, and John Wick but fully original. Dark robe/cape silhouette, staff held like a martial weapon, precise controlled spell gesture, subtle blue arcane glow, serious face mostly shadowed, powerful but restrained. Premium old RPG cover / fantasy poster mood. Panel 2: side-readable background concept for "Blackhaven / Burning Village": dark village rooftops, burning orange windows, smoky red moon, layered parallax silhouettes, readable horizontal combat space in the lower third, elegant dark fantasy color composition, not overly detailed in the play area. Panel 3: enemy silhouette lineup on a neutral dark ground: small pale skeleton, slow greenish zombie, fast pink-violet ghoul, large broad dark ogre. Shapes must be instantly distinguishable for gameplay. Include rough scale difference, with the ogre much larger and wider. Style: painterly premium fantasy illustration, moody cinematic lighting, strong silhouettes, controlled orange firelight plus cold blue magic accents, readable composition, high contrast, serious mature tone. Avoid anime style, avoid cartoon proportions, avoid pixel art, avoid cute/mobile UI aesthetics. No text labels in the image.

### Background Prompt

Create a wide 2D side-view game background concept for "Blackhaven / Burning Village" for a dark fantasy action roguelite. No characters, no UI, no text. 16:9 composition, premium painterly fantasy illustration, not pixel art, not mobile-game style. Requirements: side-readable horizontal combat scene with the lower third kept relatively open and low-detail for gameplay readability. Layered parallax feeling: foreground dark ground plane, midground burned village houses and broken fences, background rooftops, smoke, red-orange moon or fire glow behind silhouettes. Burning windows and controlled ember light, smoky sky, cool blue shadows. Elegant, mature, atmospheric, old RPG cover / fantasy poster mood. Strong silhouettes but not cluttered. Leave clear readable lanes for enemies and player. Palette: deep charcoal, dark wine red, ember orange, muted ash brown, subtle cold blue accents. Avoid over-detail in the active combat area; avoid cartoon/anime/cute/mobile aesthetics.

### Mage Concept Prompt

Create a full-body single character concept for a battle-hardened dark fantasy mage for a 2D side-view action roguelite. Transparent or plain dark neutral background, no text, no UI, no environment. The character should be readable as an in-game sprite reference. Character: serious, controlled, stylish combat mage, inspired by the calm tactical presence of Neo/Morpheus/John Wick but fully original and in a grim fantasy world. No wizard hat, no silly robe, no anime posing. Dark long coat/robe/cape silhouette, armored subtle details, staff held like a martial weapon, one hand making a precise spell gesture. Slight side-facing 3/4 pose suitable for side-view combat. Subtle blue arcane glow around hand/staff, dark cape shape strong and readable. Mature premium fantasy cover style, painterly but clean silhouette, high contrast edges, elegant black/charcoal cloth with muted brass and cold blue accents. Avoid cartoon proportions, avoid mobile-game gloss, avoid pixel art.

### Enemy Sheet Prompt

Create a dark fantasy enemy silhouette sprite concept sheet for a 2D side-view action roguelite. Four enemy types standing on a neutral dark ground, no text labels, no UI, no background scenery, no gore. Painterly but shape-readable, suitable as reference for placeholder-to-sprite conversion. Lineup left to right with clear scale differences and strong gameplay readability: 1. Skeleton: small, pale bone silhouette, thin limbs, fast weak enemy, slightly hunched, readable skull/ribcage. 2. Zombie: slower and tougher, green-gray corpse silhouette, heavier body, shambling posture, thicker torso. 3. Ghoul: fast pressure enemy, lean pink-violet shadow creature, low aggressive stance, long arms, sharp motion-ready silhouette. 4. Ogre: very large broad dark brute, massive shoulders and arms, slow heavy unit, early-background silhouette must be unmistakable. Style: premium dark fantasy concept art, mature old RPG cover tone, controlled colors, high contrast silhouettes, minimal detail inside shapes, clear side-view game readability. Avoid cute, anime, cartoon, mobile-game gloss, pixel art, excessive armor detail, or busy backgrounds.
